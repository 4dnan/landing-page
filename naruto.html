Summary

Naruto is about a young kid named Naruto Uzumaki who was used as a vessel to contain the nine tailed fox who wrecked havoc around Konoha village. Feared because of the beast inside him, Naruto was an outcast who often pulled pranks to get reactions from villagers but decided to follow in his father's footsteps and become Hokage(leader of the village) so he could be acknowledged by everyone.
